Develop an online learning platform where students can take courses and instructors can create content.

Student features:
- Browse and enroll in courses
- Watch video lectures
- Take quizzes and assessments
- Download course materials
- Earn certificates upon completion
- Participate in discussion forums
- Track learning progress

Instructor features:
- Create and publish courses
- Upload video content
- Create quizzes and assignments
- Monitor student progress and engagement
- Respond to student questions
- Generate reports on course performance

The platform must support video streaming with adaptive bitrate. It should have a discussion forum for each course. Payment processing is needed for paid courses. The design should be professional and education-focused.

Platform: Web-based, must work on desktop and tablet devices. Video player should support subtitles and playback speed control.
